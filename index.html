Introducing Voice Message Transcripts


Sending a voice message makes connecting with friends and familyeven more personal. There’s something special about hearing yourloved one’s voice even when you’re far away. Though sometimes,you’re on the move, in a loud place, or you receive a long voice message that you just can’t stop and listen. For those momentswe’re excited to introduce voice message transcripts. Voice messages can be transcribed into text to help you keep up with conversations no matter what you’re doing.
